Business Problem Statement:¶

We are using the data to get valuable insights that can we used to make important decisions. This information will also be used to find areas to improve. This includes maximing profits and minizing loses. We are also finding if we can increase the efficiency in the process of delivering to the customers. We can find out areas that are in the reachable areas as well.¶
